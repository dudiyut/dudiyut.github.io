Our Mission

At its core, our startup is dedicated to a foundational mission of the modern internet: organizing the world's information to make it universally accessible and useful. We are in the active development phase of a next-generation search engine, designed to provide more relevant, comprehensive, and faster search results. This ambitious undertaking involves building a sophisticated distributed system that navigates the public web, much like the pioneering search engines of the past, but with a fresh perspective aimed at addressing the information challenges of today.

Information Security Commitment

As an integral part of our development and data collection process, our systems perform automated HTTP requests to publicly accessible web servers. These interactions are the lifeblood of any search engine, allowing us to index content so it can be discovered by users. We want to unequivocally state that this activity is strictly non-malicious; it consists of legitimate, standard web traffic identical to that of a typical browser. We do not engage in port scanning, vulnerability exploitation, or any aggressive scanning techniques. Our focus is solely on parsing and understanding the open web, and we are committed to operating in a respectful and responsible manner.
